### Composition (30% weight)
- Rule of thirds, leading lines, patterns
- Balance, symmetry, visual flow
- Foreground/background relationships
- Use of negative space
- Framing and cropping decisions

### Image Quality (25% weight)
- Sharpness and detail (where artistically appropriate)
- Noise management
- Dynamic range and exposure
- Color accuracy and vibrancy
- Post-processing quality

### Commercial Sellability (25% weight)
- Market demand for subject/style
- Uniqueness vs oversaturation
- Technical requirements met
- Print reproduction quality
- Broad vs niche appeal

### Artistic Merit (20% weight)
- Creative vision and originality
- Emotional impact and storytelling
- Mastery of photographic techniques
- Personal style development
- Artistic risk-taking

## PLACEMENT LOGIC
- PORTFOLIO: High artistic merit (7+) regardless of commercial appeal
